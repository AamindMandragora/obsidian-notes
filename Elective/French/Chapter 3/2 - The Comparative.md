In French, we can express the idea of more than, less than, or equality between two nouns or groups in terms of adjectives (which must agree with the noun they're modifying), adverbs, verbs (which must be conjugated), and nouns using the comparative.

| Part of Speech | Supériorité              | Égalité                   | Infériorité               |
| -------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| adjectives     | plus [adjectif.ve.s] que | aussi [adjectif.ve.s] que | moins [adjectif.ve.s] que |
| adverbs        | plus [adverbe] que       | aussi [adverbe] que       | moins [adverbe] que       |
| verbs          | [verbe] plus que         | [verbe] autant que        | [verbe] moins que         |
| nouns          | plus de [nom]            | autant de [nom]           | moins de [nom]            |

"Bon / mauvais / bien" have special comparative forms for "more than".

| Part of Speech | French      | Superiorité      |
| -------------- | ----------- | ---------------- |
| adjective      | bon.ne.s    | meilleur.e.s que |
|                | mauvais.e.s | pire.s que       |
| adverbe        | bien        | mieux que        |
