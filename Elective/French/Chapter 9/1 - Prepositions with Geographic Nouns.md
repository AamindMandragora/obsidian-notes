| Case                                  | In / To / At | From    |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------ | ------- |
| feminine countries                    | en           | de / d' |
| masculine countries (consonant start) | au           | du      |
| masculine countries (vowel start)     | en           | d'      |
| plural countries                      | aux          | des     |
| cities and islands                    | à            | de      |

Most feminine countries and states all end in "e". Most cities and islands don't have articles, but there are a few exceptions. For islands with articles, use "en" instead of "à".
