Typically, words ending in "-age", "-ment", "-eau", "-phone", "-scope", "-isme" are masculine, and words ending in "-tion", "-sion", "-té", "-ette", "-ance", "-ence", "-ie", "-ure", "-ode", "-ade", "-ude" are typically feminine.

Plural forms of nouns typically add an "-s". However, if they end in "-s", "-x", or "-z" in the singular, they don't change. Nouns ending in "-al", "-ail", "-au", "-eu", "-eau", "-ou" will end in "-x" in the plural.
