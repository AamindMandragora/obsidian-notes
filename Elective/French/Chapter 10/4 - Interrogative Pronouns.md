To introduce questions about people, objects, or ideas, use an interrogative pronoun.

|        | Subject          | Direct Object    | Object of Preposition |
| ------ | ---------------- | ---------------- | --------------------- |
| People | qui (est-ce qui) | qui (est-ce que) | de qui (est-ce que)   |
| Ideas  | qu'est-ce qui    | que (est-ce que) | a quoi (est-ce que)   |

There are several ways to ask "which one(s)" in French.

| Form      | Singular             | Plural                |
| --------- | -------------------- | --------------------- |
| Normal    | lequel / laquelle    | lesquels / lesquelles |
| With "à"  | auquel / à laquelle  | auxquels / auxquelles |
| With "de" | duquel / de laquelle | desquels / desquelles |
